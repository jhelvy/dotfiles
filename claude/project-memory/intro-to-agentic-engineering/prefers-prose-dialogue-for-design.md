For open-ended course/design decisions, John prefers to talk things through in
prose rather than answer structured multiple-choice prompts.

**Why:** He twice declined the `AskUserQuestion` structured-question UI early in a
course-design session, choosing to "clarify" and reason through the options
conversationally instead.

**How to apply:** When working through design/architecture choices with him,
present a clear recommendation plus honest tradeoffs in prose and ask focused
questions inline. Reserve the structured question tool for genuinely discrete,
mutually exclusive choices — don't default to it for exploratory thinking.
